Mike R - South California

"Alexia has an amazing ability to connect with others, and this power she has, from my experience, and talking with others who’ve been a part of her guided meditations, allows us to go within, and often surprisingly to points I hadn’t really experienced before. It’s not just the emotional aspect of her guided meditations that’s been profound, it’s also a focus on oneself in a way that feels fairly rare. I really appreciate maybe most of all her ability to get you to see other angles, to see other viewpoints without making you feel uncomfortable. That’s a great gift, and a really difficult thing for most people to do. But she also makes everything fun — she has a great sense of humor, and is very playful at times, which also helps others connect to her. I think she’s a great spiritual teacher and a guide that makes meditation and a focus on going within not only something to look forward to, but exciting as well."

Bette C - Delaware

"While I have been meditating for years, I was fortunate to discovered Alexia Cabbadias, an authentic meditation practitioner and spiritual advocate teacher, a few years ago. This mediation sharing with her has deeply enhanced my own practice. Her compassionate intention is to help one navigate oneself with her guided meditations to aid one to be in the moment with our natural breathing, taking one into their own unique awareness, without self judgement if one’s mind should wander. Her softly calming and lovingly voice guides one through a natural essence of being, in a trusted slowing stillness, quiet listening of letting go, and a peaceful mindful state. All I have experienced with Alexia has brought me to a blossoming that transformed me from my anxieties, fear, self doubt and emotional challenges. I recommend her highly for anyone who wants to go inward as a tool for a better benefical way of living."
